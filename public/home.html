About Subscription Ghost

Subscription Ghost is a professional financial tool designed to alleviate subscription fatigue. By centralizing your recurring expenses, we help you maintain control over your digital financial footprint.

Core Functionality

  • Intelligent Tracking: Leverages Google Gemini AI to retrieve real-time pricing and trial duration data.
  • Automated Alerts: Proactive email notifications sent 3 days prior to trial expiration.
  • Market Intelligence: Weekly curated reports on price changes and new offers.
  • Financial Analytics: comprehensive breakdown of monthly obligations and historical spending.

Capstone Project v1.0.0 | Tech Stack: Node.js, Express, MongoDB, Google Gemini AI
